Oliveira was Founded: 1962

Manuel Oliveira, who owned the site and grew orange groves, had it planted in his honor. A granddaughter, Joan Fragus, recently retired from her teaching position in the Fremont Unified School District. A great granddaughter, Trish Nunes, currently teaches in the district, and a great great granddaughter, Emily Blackburn, is on the staff of Oliveira School. The tradition continues.

School Achievements

Won the Golden Sneaker Contest
Oliveira Wins 'Platinum Sneaker' Award

The 2018 Alameda County Safe Routes to Schools Program Platinum Sneaker Contest was won by Oliveira Elementary School in Fremont, California. Oliveira was honored during the Alameda CTC Commission Meeting on April 26th. During the Golden Sneaker Contest, the Platinum Sneaker Award is given to the school in Alameda County that experienced the biggest percent increase in students using "green" ways to go to school.
